Life has been a struggle over the last few months. My initial reflection on this meditation was focused on how I should respond, but after reading it several times, I realized that the message is about preparation.

If we prepare ourselves for what is to come, it will be easier to deal with. Create a morning ritual, a tiny habit. When you wake up, before you put your feet on the ground, remind yourself that today is going to be a good day. Others may treat us poorly, but we have the power to react with tolerance, mercy, and dignity.

### Conclusion

Start your day with the [tiny habit](https://tinyhabits.com/) of affirmation. Before you let your feet hit the floor, commit to having a good day. Prepare your mind for the fact that not everyone will treat you well, and remember that being kind in return costs you nothing.
